If you are interested in joining the lab, please send a concise email describing your research interests, relevant experience, and why you are interested in our group. Strong candidates have a background in robotics, AI, computer vision, or embedded systems.

Before reaching out, prospective PhD students should read the PI's research statement and complete the interest form. Please include your CV, transcript, a statement of research interests, and any relevant experience or publications.

Prospective student form

Prospective PhD students

We recruit PhD students through the University of South Florida. Ideal applicants have a strong research record and a background in robotics, AI, or embedded systems. Please apply through the form above and review the PI's research statement before contacting.

Master's students

USF Master's students interested in robotics and perception research are welcome to reach out with a CV and a short description of your interests.

Undergraduate researchers

USF undergraduates looking for hands-on research experience in robotics, computer vision, or machine learning are encouraged to email the PI with your interests and availability.

Collaborators

We welcome collaboration in robotics, AI, edge computing, and marine, agricultural, and healthcare applications. Please get in touch to discuss joint projects.
