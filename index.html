Embodied Robotics & Autonomy · University of South Florida

Intelligent robots that perceive, reason, and act in the real world.

The ERA Lab develops intelligent robotic systems for complex, real-world environments. Our research spans computer vision, robot perception, multimodal learning, autonomous systems, and embodied AI — with applications in underwater robotics, field robotics, and intelligent autonomy.
